About Us
Our
Objectives
At OverKill, we strive in maintaining the integrity of our four main objectives, outlined to the right.
01
Organization
We take pride in our organization. Utilizing a strong backbone of intelligent staff members to maintain continuity throughout the entire clan, we are given the opportunity to plan and execute events. Whether its an in-game raid, or a pre-wipe meeting, we ensure clarity and decisiveness.
02
Sophistication
OverKill isn't your typical Rust clan. Instead of promoting a lawless environment that breeds chaos, we strictly enforce rules that ensure the positivity and benefit of anyone willing to put in the effort to make the clan a better place. We have zero tolerance for racism, sexism, or any other behavior that negatively affects anyone's experience.
03
Dominance
We are undeniably an unstoppable force. Through our consistent success in main-scene raids and numerous memorable wipes, OverKill's name reverberates strongly with dominancee.
04
Skill
OverKill can be for everyone. We ensure succesful wipes and a positive environment through the blunt and technical skill of our members. Don't have intense PvP skills but are a Nikola Tesla with the electrical system? Overkill is the clan for you. To put it simply, OverKill contains some of the best Rust players.
